I built a custom 3D printer based on an Ender 3 and the Switchwire concept, aiming to make it modular, compact, and buildable by others using small-format printers.

The project gave me hands-on experience with firmware tuning, motion system calibration, and designing around real-world manufacturing limits.

Printing Layer: 0/100

Hardware Stack

  • Base: Ender 3 + Switchwire Gantry
  • Custom frame parts, many 3D printed
  • Raspberry Pi + BTT Octopus
  • Klipper firmware with custom macros

Design Goals

  • Small part footprint for accessibility
  • Printable components for other hobbyists
  • Swappable electronics for modularity
  • Firmware-first development and testing
